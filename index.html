Canary Islands

I am from Spain. More specifically, Tenerife, in the Canary Islands. They are also known as the Fortunates or Fortunate Islands because of their mild climate, beautiful ocean scenary and astonishing volcanos. Tenerife is crowned by Teide, the third biggest volcano from the ocean floor (7500m). For the Guanches (the indiginious group from the Island) it was a sacred Mountain, similar to Mount Olympus for the Greeks
